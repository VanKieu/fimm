Identify Faces

Face attributes extraction

Face detection

Find the face fragments in an image based on the control points (eyes, nose, mouth), then convert them to data to identify the face.

Comepare Faces

Compare the captured face with each face in the crime profile data, you will get characteristics and ratio to gauge similarity.

Similar Faces

Displaying faces with a high similarity ratio to the provided image then helps you find images that are homogeneous.

Video/Webcam

In addition to upload photos, you can upload video or photograph from the webcam to identify faces.
